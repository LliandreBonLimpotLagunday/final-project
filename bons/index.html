Welcome!

We raise awareness and provide solutions for global clean water and sanitation issues.

Effects

Contaminated water and poor sanitation are linked to diseases such as cholera, hepatitis A, typhoid, and more.

Solutions

1. Educate to change consumption and lifestyles

Change comes from education. Water scarcity requires individual and corporate reform.

2. Recycle wastewater

Countries like Singapore recycle wastewater to reduce reliance on imports.

3. How You Can Help

  • Donate to water projects.
  • Spread awareness about water scarcity.
  • Volunteer for clean water initiatives.

Statistics

703 million people lack clean water. Women and girls spend 200 million hours daily fetching water.
